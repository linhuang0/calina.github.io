Aliexpress web development

JAVA MAVEN SCRUM PROJECT MANAGENENT API

Requirements :

In the context of AliExpress website and mobile app development, the primary requirement was to enhance and optimize the user experience by improving key modules. This involved addressing specific needs such as:
  • Shopping Cart Module: The requirement was to refine the shopping cart functionality, ensuring that it provided a seamless and efficient experience for users when managing their purchases.
  • Product Evaluation Module: Another important requirement was to develop and enhance the product evaluation system, enabling customers to leave reviews and ratings for products with ease.
  • Mobile App Integration: For the mobile platform, the requirement was to create mobile APIs that could seamlessly connect different modules and departments to ensure the smooth operation of the AliExpress mobile app.

Objectives :

  • To improve the functionality of the shopping cart, making it user-friendly and efficient.
  • To enhance the product evaluation system, encouraging users to provide valuable feedback.
  • To create and integrate mobile APIs that facilitate communication between various modules and departments, ensuring the success of the AliExpress mobile app.

Approach :

To achieve these objectives, I adopted a systematic and collaborative approach:
  • Development and Refinement: I engaged in the development and refinement of the shopping cart module on the AliExpress website, focusing on optimizing the user interface and ensuring a smooth shopping experience.
  • Enhancing Product Evaluation: I worked on enhancing the product evaluation module, implementing features that encouraged customers to leave reviews and ratings, thereby enriching the shopping experience for others.
  • API Development: In the realm of mobile app development, I closely collaborated with different departments to design and create mobile APIs that seamlessly integrated various functionalities, such as sales promotion, evaluations, messaging, and email delivery.
  • Testing and Integration:** Throughout the project, I conducted rigorous testing to ensure the reliability and functionality of the developed modules and APIs. Integration with the existing systems was a crucial step.
Achievements :

As a result of these efforts:
  1. The shopping cart module on the AliExpress website became more user-friendly, leading to increased user engagement and improved sales.
  2. The product evaluation system saw greater user participation, providing valuable insights and feedback for both customers and sellers.
  3. The mobile APIs I developed and integrated played a pivotal role in the success of the AliExpress mobile app, offering users a seamless and feature-rich mobile shopping experience.
Overall, my contributions directly contributed to the optimization and enrichment of the AliExpress platform, benefiting both users and the business.
